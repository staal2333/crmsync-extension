## Step 3: Start Using It!

### First Time Setup
1. **Click the Extension Icon** (in Chrome toolbar, top-right)
   - This opens the settings popup
   - Configure your preferences:
     - Dark Mode: Toggle if you prefer dark theme
     - Auto Approve: Turn ON to skip approval prompts
     - Enable Sidebar: Keep ON to see the dashboard
     - Reminder Days: Set how many days before follow-up reminders

### How It Works

**Automatic Contact Detection:**
- Open any email thread in Gmail
- The extension automatically scans for email addresses
- When a new contact is found, an approval panel appears (top-right)

**Using the Dashboard:**
- Click the **floating widget** (bottom-right corner) to open the sidebar
- View your contact stats and recent contacts
- Export your contacts as CSV with one click

## Troubleshooting

**Extension not working?**
- Make sure you're on Gmail (mail.google.com)
- Refresh the Gmail page (F5)
- Check the browser console (F12 â†’ Console) for any errors
- Make sure the extension is enabled in chrome://extensions/

**Not detecting contacts?**
- Open an email thread (click on an email to view it)
- The extension scans emails as you view them
- Try sending or receiving a new email
- Check console (F12) for "CRMSYNC: Ready!" message

**Sidebar not showing?**
- Click the floating widget (bottom-right)
- Or use "View Dashboard" button in the extension popup
- Make sure "Enable Sidebar" is ON in settings
