Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: CRMSYNC is committed to protecting your privacy. We never read your email content. We only access email metadata (sender names and addresses) to help you manage contacts.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how CRMSYNC ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension and related services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account
  • Contact Data: Names, email addresses, companies, and job titles that you choose to save
  • Exclusion Rules: Filters and settings you configure to control what contacts are captured
  • CRM Credentials: OAuth tokens for HubSpot and Salesforce (stored securely)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Email Metadata: Sender names, email addresses, and timestamps from Gmail headers
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the extension (features used, settings changed)
  • Technical Data: Browser type, extension version, error logs

2.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • ❌ Email content or message bodies
  • ❌ Email attachments
  • ❌ Email subjects
  • ❌ Your Gmail password
  • ❌ Credit card information (handled by Stripe)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Detect and extract contact information from Gmail
  • Sync contacts to your connected CRM platforms
  • Provide cloud backup of your contact database
  • Improve our AI detection algorithms
  • Send service notifications and updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Process payments (via Stripe)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your data. We only share your information in these limited cases:

4.1 Service Providers

  • Stripe: Payment processing (never stores full card numbers)
  • Render.com: Cloud hosting and database
  • HubSpot/Salesforce: When you choose to sync contacts to these CRMs

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request.

4.3 Business Transfers

If we're acquired or merged, your data may transfer to the new owner (you'll be notified).

5. Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Database encryption at rest
  • OAuth 2.0 for CRM connections
  • No plaintext password storage (bcrypt hashing)
  • Regular security audits
  • GDPR and CCPA compliant

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update incorrect information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all data
  • Export: Download your contacts as CSV
  • Opt-Out: Disable cloud sync (local-only mode)
  • Portability: Transfer data to another service

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data stored as long as account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Deleted after 12 months of inactivity
  • Cancelled Subscriptions: Data retained for 30 days, then deleted
  • Logs: Technical logs kept for 90 days

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies:

  • Authentication Cookie: Keeps you logged in
  • Session Cookie: Temporary, expires when browser closes
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We don't use Google Analytics or similar

9. Chrome Extension Permissions

We request these permissions and use them only for stated purposes:

  • storage: Save contacts and settings locally
  • activeTab: Detect when Gmail is open
  • scripting: Inject UI into Gmail
  • mail.google.com: Access Gmail to detect contacts

10. Children's Privacy

CRMSYNC is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States and European Union. We use standard contractual clauses to protect international transfers.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with a new "Last Updated" date. Continued use means you accept the changes.

14. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you're in the EU, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to be forgotten (complete data deletion)
  • Right to data portability (export in machine-readable format)
  • Right to object to automated decision-making
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your local DPA

15. CCPA Compliance (California Users)

If you're a California resident:

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • You can opt-out of data sharing with third parties
  • You can request disclosure of what data we collect
  • You can request deletion of your data
